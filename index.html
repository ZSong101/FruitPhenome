Step 1: Select your fruit

This walkthrough configures your analysis step by step: select a fruit type, answer a few questions about your images, then review your setup. Once finished, move to Single Image Analysis or Bulk Image Analysis to schedule a processing job. See the Help section at the bottom of this page for explanations of each feature.

Select Fruit is required before continuing.

Step 2: Scale and color calibration

ColorChecker reference: use the middle page of the Calibrite ColorChecker Passport Photo 2, the 24-patch page that looks like this reference image.

Scale priority: ArUco marker, then ColorChecker, then manual scale, then pixel units.

Step 3: On-screen labels
